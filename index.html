Session Recap

The Last Word

The heat of the sun, coupled with the stillness of the air, weighs on you oppressively. The sparse cries of seagulls punctuate the steady, lazy lapping of waves against the ship on which you are passengers. The salt air mingles with the sweat of sailors. You were hired to protect The Last Word and its cargo by a group of Filian Wizards. It is day ten.

Our party of adventurous mercenaries noticed strange behavior from water elementals, as well as an unusual bioluminescent algae bloom in the waters as night approached. The Wizard Oneth of Filia, revealed some of the precious cargo The Last Word carried, which included several "Candidates" - rare magical Artifacts - a silvery greatsword, a pair of daggers given the name The Wren and the Nightingale and a mace with a bell-shaped head.
